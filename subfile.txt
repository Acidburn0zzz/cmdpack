subfile(1)
==========
:doctype: manpage

NAME
----
subfile - extract a portion of a file

SYNOPSIS
--------
*subfile* 'infile' 'offset' 'length' 'outfile'

DESCRIPTION
-----------
Extracts a portion of a file, given a starting offset and length in
bytes.  Offset and length may be specified in C style ``0x123'' or
``0123'' notation for hexadecimal or octal.
