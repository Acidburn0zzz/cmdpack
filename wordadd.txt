wordadd(1)
==========
:doctype: manpage

NAME
----
wordadd - Addition word puzzle solver

SYNOPSIS
--------
*wordadd* 'words'... 'sum'

DESCRIPTION
-----------
Finds solutions to letter/digit substitution of the ``WORD + WORD =
OTHER'' variety.  Supports multiple adends and digits mixed in with
letters.

Example: wordadd BEEF BACON MEATS
